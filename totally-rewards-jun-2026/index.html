Why we've approached this the way we have
Your filed accounts show a period of investment and growth — and on paper they look loss-making. But your management accounts to March 2026 tell a very different story: over £1 million in turnover and a profitable EBITDA. That's a 327% increase in revenue in under two years.

We've focused on lenders who either look primarily at current trading rather than historic filed accounts, or who have a more holistic approach to underwriting. The lenders who rely solely on filed accounts aren't on this list because they won't give you the answer you deserve.

The revenue-based options on this list are particularly well suited to Totally's model — recurring B2B invoice revenue, international clients, and strong monthly inflows are exactly the profile those lenders are built for. We expect the most competitive offers to come from that route.
