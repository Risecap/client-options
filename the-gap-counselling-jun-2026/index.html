When to come back to us
The Gap Counselling is a good business. Here's how to put it in a stronger borrowing position.
Focus on these over the next 6-12 months and you'll have significantly more options available to you.
01
Build your monthly revenue consistency
Your filed turnover tells a great story, but lenders also look at what's flowing through your account month to month. Getting your average monthly revenue above £5,000 consistently - and keeping it there for 3-6 months - opens up a much wider pool of lenders, including those who offer faster decisions and better rates.
Target: 6 months of consistent trading
02
Grow the account balance cushion
Lenders check your average end-of-day account balance as a health signal. A stronger average balance - ideally a month's worth of outgoings - shows them the business isn't operating right on the edge. Even a modest improvement here makes a real difference to how applications are received.
Target: Average balance 1x monthly costs
03
Let the credit history age
The previous company history is the main thing holding this back with traditional lenders. With every month of clean, positive trading under The Gap Counselling Ltd, that history carries less weight. Most lenders we work with look back 2-3 years - once the current business has a stronger track record behind it, the doors open considerably.
Target: 2 years clean trading history
04
Build on your next set of filed accounts
Your 94% gross margin and increasing revenue trend are genuinely strong. Once your Sep 2026 accounts are filed - if they continue that trajectory - you'll have two years of growing, profitable accounts to put in front of lenders. That's a very different conversation, and the amount you can access grows substantially with it.
Target: Sep 2026 accounts filed
